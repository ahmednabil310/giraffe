What is the Giraffe
platform?

Giraffe is not just a Video Management System even though it does that brilliantly.

The Giraffe Platform is an easy-to-use cloud based remote tower management system capable of being operated by any internet enabled device, anywhere.

From a single holistic dashboard, you can view all your cameras, be alerted to any intrusions and see the overall health of your remote CCTV tower.

We believe your tower should be easy to setup, control and manage and that is exactly what the Giraffe Platform allows you to do.

From an internet browser you can view or play back your cameras, control the siren, speaker, floodlight & strobe from the same screen as managing your team permissions. You can view all your towers from different sites in a single location.

Better Analytics

Are you fed up of false detections from your “AI”?

These are often caused as the processors in the built in analytics on board most cameras mean they have to do the analytics on a low resolution image. It might work in a lab but not on a buliding site in the rain 6m up in the air.

Our analytics are done onboard using a state of the art Google Coral Processor, designed for the purpose of AI and people detection. No longer do you have to put up with false detections when a traffic cone is blown across the image. Only get detections when you want them making it more likely you’ll catch someone when you really need to.

Cloud Connected Recording

The Giraffe Platform has a state of the art hybrid recording platform built in. Not only can you view your cameras live but you can play them back and download any of the footage in HD from any internet connection in any internet brow g-0ser

The footage is stored locally on the tower but is played back through the cloud giving you ultimate flexibility to view your recordings from anywhere without the cost of storing them in the cloud. If you would like cloud back up, we do this too.

No longer do you have to wait hours trying to find an event.
See tower events on the timeline and quickly review snapshots to drill down into exactly what happened and when.

Alarms and Automation

You can control the tower manually, but it’s more powerful if it can take automated actions when it senses something is amiss.

Battery operated PIR sensors can trigger PTZ presets which will allow you to cover a much larger area with a single tower.

The AI can trigger siren, strobe, floodlight or speaker activations when a vehicle or person is detected moving as well as sending alerts.

Schedules can be configured to automatically arm and disarm the system, or turn the floodlights on overnight.
