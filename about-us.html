WHO ARE WE?

Created in 2012, Rugged Networks is a technology company that has focussed on the development of software for the remote management of IP cameras in challenging locations. Having expanded into product design and assembly, we have sought to create a market leading product for CCTV towers.

Present

From a single holistic dashboard, Giraffe have now created a product that means you can view all of your remote cameras, be alerted to any intrusions. record and play back incidents as well as see the overall health of your CCTV tower. The Giraffe Platform is an easy-to-use cloud based remote tower management system.

We believe a CCTV tower should be simplle to setup, control and manage and that is exactly what the Giraffe Platform allows you to do.

In line with an ethos of brilliant simplicity and it must work “out of the box” we also believe that it should be easy to assemble. That is why we don’t just focus on the software.
